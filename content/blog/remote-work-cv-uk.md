Remote and hybrid roles are standard across UK tech, marketing, finance, and admin. Employers no longer assume remote work — they look for evidence you can deliver outcomes without office oversight. Your CV must prove digital fluency, communication, and reliability.

## What Recruiters Look for on Remote CVs

- **Proven remote or hybrid experience** — dates, tools, outcomes
- **Digital collaboration tools** — Slack, Teams, Zoom, Asana, Jira
- **Written communication** — async updates, documentation, client emails
- **Self-management** — meeting deadlines without daily supervision
- **Home office setup** (sometimes) — reliable broadband, UK timezone availability

## How to Show Remote Experience

If you worked remotely, say so in the job entry:

*"Marketing Coordinator — Hybrid (3 days remote) | Acme Ltd | Jan 2022 – Present"*

Bullets should highlight remote-specific wins:

- *"Led weekly stand-ups via Microsoft Teams for 12-person distributed team across UK and EU timezones"*
- *"Documented SOPs in Notion, reducing onboarding questions by 40% for remote hires"*

No remote history? Emphasise traits and tools: project ownership, video calls, CRM, cloud software.

## Skills to Include for Remote Roles

Microsoft Teams · Slack · Zoom · Google Workspace · Asana · Trello · Time Management · Written Communication · CRM · VPN / Remote Desktop

Add to your [skills section](/blog/cv-skills-section-guide-uk) — do not rely on icons or badges ATS cannot read.

## Location on Remote CVs

UK employers often hire remote workers within the UK for tax and legal reasons. State your city or region clearly:

*"Based in Manchester, UK — available for hybrid (2 days office) or fully remote roles"*

Avoid listing a false location if the role requires UK residency.

## Remote CV Personal Statement Example

*"Operations coordinator with 3 years hybrid experience supporting distributed teams. Expert in Microsoft Teams, Excel reporting, and async stakeholder updates. Seeking fully remote admin or PMO roles with UK-based employers."*

More examples in our [personal statement guide](/blog/cv-personal-statement-examples-uk).

## Industries With High Remote Volume in the UK

- Tech and SaaS (developers, product, customer success)
- Digital marketing and content
- Finance and accounting (hybrid common)
- Customer support (home-based shifts)
- Project management and consulting

Tailor CV keywords to each sector. Tech roles want Jira and Git; support roles want Zendesk and SLA metrics.

## ATS for Remote Job Boards

Remote listings on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Reed still use ATS. Use [ATS-friendly templates](/templates/ats-friendly-cv-template) and mirror remote keywords: "self-starter," "distributed team," "home-based," "hybrid."

Read [top ATS resume tips](/blog/top-ats-resume-tips) before bulk applying.

## Remote Interview Follow-Up

Your CV gets you the video interview. Ensure contact details include:
- Professional email checked daily
- Mobile number with UK dialling code
- LinkedIn URL with updated headline mentioning remote availability

## Hybrid vs Fully Remote Applications

Do not send a "fully remote only" CV to a strict office role — tailor personal statement per posting. Hybrid employers want flexibility; fully remote employers want async communication proof.
