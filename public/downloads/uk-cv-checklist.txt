Use this checklist before every job application.

CONTACT & FORMAT
[ ] Name, mobile, professional email, city on the CV
[ ] British English spelling (organise, centre, programme)
[ ] 1–2 pages; reverse chronological order
[ ] No photo or date of birth unless requested
[ ] Month/year dates (Jan 2022 – Present)

CONTENT
[ ] Personal statement tailored to the role (3–4 lines)
[ ] Work experience with achievement-focused bullets
[ ] Numbers in bullets where possible (KPIs, targets, volumes)
[ ] Skills section with 8–12 keywords from the job ad
[ ] Education section with most recent qualification first

ATS & EXPORT
[ ] Standard headings: Summary, Experience, Education, Skills
[ ] No tables, text boxes, or skill bar graphics
[ ] Plain-text PDF export tested on another device
[ ] Keywords from job description included naturally

FINAL CHECK
[ ] Proofread twice; zero typos
[ ] References line: "Available on request"
[ ] File named professionally: Firstname-Lastname-CV.pdf
